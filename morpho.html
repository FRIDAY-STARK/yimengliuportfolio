"MORPHO"
Logo Design & Motion Graphics
Logo of MORPHO, a photography studio.
“MORPHO” is a portrait photography studio that has everyday and commercial branches. MORPHO shoot not only for family, couples, friends, and, of course, individuals, but also for fashion studios and professional models.

Morpho is a genus of butterfly, which usually looks glittering. Butterfly is fleeting, so are time and memories, yet photography is about capturing the moment. We hope the memories recorded by our lenses could be as bright and shiny as the wings of morpho. The overall aesthetic of the everyday branch is dreamy and nostalgic, while that of the commercial branch is sharp and cool.

After designing the static logo in Adobe Illustrator, I made an animated version for the commercial branch in Adobe After Effects. The intro sound effect is created in REAPER.



Iterations
For the final version, I continued on the two visual directions - sharp and round, though they shared the same skeleton of butterfly. I compared the two styles with the features of target customers and value propositions of the two different branches, and based on the results of compare and contrast, I made my final decisions.



MORPHO: Everyday Branch
The round shape makes it look more accessible and approachable. The combination and the overlap of blue with different saturations conveys the inclusiveness of the everyday branch of MORPHO. It resonates with what the brand advocates - portrait photography is not just for fashion industry and professional models, but for everyone.
The choice of typeface was also considerate. Since the majority colors of the graphics are light and somewhat transparent, to anchor the butterfly, I have chosen a solid font. It not only makes the brand name look more reliable and legible, but also prevents the morpho from fleeting - preserves people’s memories from losing.





MORPHO: Commercial Branch
The wings of butterfly is depicted in stars that give the logo a sharp feeling, making it fit the impression of fashion industry. The design is mimicking the flashlights of camera when pressing shutters. It metaphorically reveals how bright and shiny models are in MORPHO's camera, and it also expresses MORPHO's wish for their customers to become famous in their field.
To match the feeling of being clean and sharp, I picked a relatively thin font and changed the "O"s in "MORPHO" to two thin stars to resonate with the graphics.
