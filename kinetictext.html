"As A Monster"
Motion Graphics - Kinetic Text
Music credit: 作為怪物 (As a Monster) by Chris Lee and Wu Tsing-fong
As a person who sometimes feels like being in another universe, I was intrigued by Chris Lee and Wu Tsing-fong's 作為怪物 (As a Monster). Since I enjoy being a weirdo and a misfit, and also most of my works are revealing my inner world, I chose to design kinetic text for this song. In this video, the kinetics and compositions are intentionally designed to express my interpretation of the lyrics and convey my feelings of the melody.

The motion graphics are made in Adobe After Effects.
