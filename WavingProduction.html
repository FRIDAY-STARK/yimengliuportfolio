"Waving Production"
Stage Photos & Behind The Scenes
Student Production of Dear Evan Hansen, Shanghai, China, 2021
In October 2020, I joined Waving Production, a non-profit student musical production team of Dear Evan Hansen. Waving Production was organized by Chinese international undergraduate students who were unable to go abroad for college and stayed in Shanghai due to the pandemic, in which I worked as the Publicity Manager and Assistant Director. Since March 2021, the casts and directing cohort have initiated offline rehearsal in Shanghai, from which I started to shoot behind the scenes and stage photos for publicising purpose. Some of the collections I'm showcasing below are also post on Waving Production's official account on social media and included in our playbill.
